Fully Dressed Use Case:

UC7.Search for transport

Use Case Name:Search transport
Use Case Id 7
Priority 7
Source
Primary Business Actory Customer
Other Participating Actors
DescriptionCustomer searches for ltransport
Preconditions A customer must have an account and be logged in
Trigger
Typical Scenario
  1. A customer requests search for transport option
  2. The system determines the customers location & brings up different forms of transport nearby
  3. The system requests the user to enter the date and location of departure & arrival
  4. The system finds all different forms of transport with the corresponding costs, times on that date doing fitting as close as possible with the customer's requirements
  5. The customer chooses a form of transport
Alternative Scenario
  1. No transport available in location specified: The system returns with the nearest available transport to the specified location
  2. Filtered locations do not return a search: The systems suggests other events in nearby locations
  3. The system cannot determine the customers location
  4. The customer chooses not to purchase a transport option
ConclusionsA customer is able to search for events on a specified date
Post ConditionsA customer can book transport
Business Rules
  • The system will only bring up events 6 months in advance
  • The system cannot bring up bygone events
Implementation Constraints
