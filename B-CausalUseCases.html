Casual Use Cases:

UC 3: Account Log-In

Use Case ID: 3
Use Case Name: Account Log-In
Success: Customer select account login, entering their email address and password. The system valids the email address and password and confirms the customer has successfully log into their account. Customer can then make bookings.
Alternative Scenario:
  1. Invalid Email: customer is asked to re-enter their username.
  2. Invalid Password: customer is asked to re-enter their password.
  3. Account does not exist: customer is asked to sign up for an account.

UC 5.1: Search for concert/performance genre

Use Case ID: 5.1
Use Case Name: Search for concert/performance genre
Success: Customer select the concert/performance genre, the system retrieve and display available concerts/performances under that genre
Alternative Scenario:
  1. Concert/Performance Genre not available: customer chooses another genre

UC 7: Search for Restaurant

Use Case ID: 7
Use Case Name: Search for Restaurant
Success: Customer selects the search restaurant option
Alternative Scenario:
  1. No Restaurant available: customer chooses a different search bundle

UC 7.1: Select Restaurant Type

Use Case ID: 7.1
Use Case Name: Select Restaurant Type
Success: Customer selects a restaurant type they want to search,e.g. italian. The system retrieve and display the available restauarnt under the selected restaurant type.
Alternative Scenario:
  1. Restaurant Type not available: customer chooses another restaurant type

UC 7.2: Book Restaurant

Use Case ID: 7.2
Use Case Name: Book Restaurant
Success: Customer makes a restaurant booking, customer select date, time and number of people
Alternative Scenario:
  1. Date not available: customer must choose another date
  2. Time not available: customer must choose another time

UC 9: Confirm Booking Details

Use Case ID: 9
Use Case Name: Confirm Booking Details
Success: Customer confirms bookings that were added to the cart. Booking that were added will be displayed.
Alternative Scenario:
  1. Cancel booking: customer does not want to go ahead with the booking and cancels it.
  2. Cart empty: there is no booking in cart so there is nothing to confim.
