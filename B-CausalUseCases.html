Casual Use Cases:

UC 3: Account Log-In

Use Case ID: 3
Use Case Name: Account Log-In
Success: Customer successfully log into their account
Alternative Scenario:
  1. Invalid Email: customer is asked to re-enter their username.
  2. Invalid Password: customer is asked to re-enter their password.

UC 5.1: Search for concert/performance genre

Use Case ID: 5.1
Use Case Name: Search for concert/performance genre
Success: Customer searches for available concert and or performance genre
Alternative Scenario:
  1. Customer selects the genre of the concert/performance, i.e Pop, Rock.
  2. The system displays the available concerts/performance according to the selected genre

UC 7: Search for Restaurant

Use Case ID: 7
Use Case Name: Search for Restaurant
Success: Customer searches for an available restaurant
Alternative Scenario:
  1. Restaurant not available: customer must choose another restaurant
  2. Quit: customer does not want to search for restaurant and quits the system.

UC 7.1: Select Cuisine Type

Use Case ID: 7.1
Use Case Name: Select Cuisine Type
Success: Customer selects a restaurant type, i.e. italian.
Alternative Scenario:
  1. Restaurant Type not available: customer must choose another restaurant type
  2. Quit: customer does not want to search for restaurant type and quits the system.

UC 7.2: Book Restaurant

Use Case ID: 7.2
Use Case Name: Book Restaurant
Success: Customer makes a restaurant booking, customer select date, time and number of people
Alternative Scenario:
  1. Date not available: customer must choose another date
  2. Time not available: customer must choose another time

UC 9: Confirm Booking Details

Use Case ID: 9
Use Case Name: Confirm Booking Details
Success: Customer confirms booking
Alternative Scenario:
  1. Cancel booking: customer does not want to go ahead with the booking and cancels it.
  2. Cart empty: there is no booking in cart so there is nothing to confim.
