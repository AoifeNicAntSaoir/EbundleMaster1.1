2. Account Log-In

Use Case Name:Account Log-In
Use Case Id 2
Priority 2
Source
Primary Business Actory Customer
Other Participating Actors System Administrator
Description A customer needs to log into their account in order to book purhcase ticket.
Preconditions A customer must already have signed up for an account
Trigger
Typical Scenario
  1. Customer enters the email address
  2. Customer enters the password
  3. The system valids the email and password
  4. The system confirms customer has suceesfully log into their account
Alternative Scenario
  1. Invalid email: customer asked to re-enter their email
  2. Invalid password: customer is asked to re-enter their password.
ConclusionsA customer log into their account
Post ConditionsA customer can now purchase or book concert ticket
