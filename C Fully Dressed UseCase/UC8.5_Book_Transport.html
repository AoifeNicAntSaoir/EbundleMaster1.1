Fully Dressed Use Case:

UC8.5: Book Transport

Use Case Name:Book Transport
Use Case Number 8.5
Priority 8
Source
Primary Business Actory Customer
Other Participating Actors System
DescriptionCustomer books transport to the concert venue.
Preconditions Customer must log on first to make a booking.
Stakeholders & Interests
Customer:
Wants to be able to book transportation on the Ebundle System
Company:
Wants to make profit for the company
System:
Wants to be make sure that customer can successfully make a booking
Typical Scenario
  1. Customer log on to their account.
  2. Customer invokes search for transport.
  3. Customer selects a type of transportation(Bus/Train).
  4. Customer selects date and arrival/deperature location.
  5. Customer selects book now.
  6. System adds the booking to cart.
Alternate Scenario
  1. The chosen type of transport is unavailable,in which case the customer must choose another type of transport or exit.
  2. The customer does not want to book transport, in which the customer can exit the system .
ConclusionsBooking has been added to cart.
Post ConditionsCustomer confirm the booking by viewing the cart.
Business Rules
  1. Customer must have an account.
  2. Customer must log on to make a booking.
Implementation Constraints
