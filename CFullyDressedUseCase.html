UC5.4: Book Concert

Use Case Name:Book Concert
Use Case Number 5.4
Priority 6
Source
Primary Business Actory Customer
Other Participating Actors System
DescriptionCustomer can book concert tickets.
Preconditions A customer must log on first to book a concert ticket.
Stakeholders & Interests
Customer:
Wants to be able to book concert tickets on the Ebundle System
Company:
Wants to make profit for the company
System:
Wants to be make sure that customer can successfully make a booking
Typical Scenario
  1. Customer log on to their account.
  2. Customer invokes Search for concert
  3. Customer selects a concert ticket
  4. Customer selects book now
  5. System the booking to cart
Alternate Scenario
  1. When tickets has been sold out, the customer will have to search for another concert or exit the system.
  2. When a concert has been cancelled customer will have to search for another concert or exit the system.
ConclusionsA customer made a purchase at EBundleMaster.
Post Conditions Customer recieves email confirmation of purchase.
Business Rules Customer must have an account and logged on to make a booking.
Implementation Constraints
