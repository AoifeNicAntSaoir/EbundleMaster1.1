UC6: Purchase concert/performance tickets

Use Case Name:Purchase concert/performance tickets
Use Case Id 6
Priority 1
Source
Primary Business Actory Customer
Other Participating Actors System
DescriptionA Customer identified a ticket he/she wishes to purchase.
Preconditions A customer must log on first to make a purchase.
Trigger
Typical Scenario
  1. Customer log on to their account.
  2. Customer selects a ticket to add to basket.
  3. Customer goes to checkout.
  4. Customer reviews their items.
  5. Customer selects pay now option.
  6. The system displays confirmation UI
  7. The system sends email confirmation of payment and eTicket to the customers regiestered email address.
Alternate Scenario
  1. When tickets has been sold out, the customer will have to search for another concert/performance ticket or exit the system.
  2. When a concert/performance has been cancelled customer will have to search for another concert/performance ticket or exit the system.
ConclusionsA customer made a purchase at EBundleMaster.
Post Conditions Customer recieves email confirmation of purchase.
Business Rules Customer must have an account and logged on to make a purchase.
Implementation Constraints

UC8: Book Transport

Use Case Name:Book Transport
Use Case Id 8
Priority 1
Source
Primary Business Actory Customer
Other Participating Actors System
DescriptionCustomer books transport to the concert venue.
Preconditions Customer must log on first to make a booking.
Trigger
Typical Scenario
  1. Customer log on to their account.
  2. Customer selects a type of transportation(Bus/Train).
  3. Customer selects time, date and location.
  4. Customer adds selected transportation to basket.
  5. Customer selects pay now option.
  6. The system displays confirmation UI
  7. The system sends email confirmation of payment and eTicket to the customers regiestered email address.
Alternate Scenario
  1. The chosen type of transport is unavailable,in which case the customer must choose another type of transport or exit.
  2. The customer does not want to book transport, in which the customer can exit the system .
ConclusionsA customer booked transportation at EBundleMaster.
Post Conditions Customer recieves email confirmation of purchase.
Business Rules
  1. Customer must have an account.
  2. Customer must log on to make a booking.
  3. In order to purchase transport ticket, customer must purchase a concert/performance ticket.
Implementation Constraints

UC11: Search Hotel Rooms

Use Case Name:Search Hotel Rooms
Use Case Id 11
Priority 1
Source
Primary Business Actory Customer
Other Participating Actors System
DescriptionCustomer can search for available hotel room.
Preconditions Customer must log on first to search hotel rooms.
Trigger
Typical Scenario
  1. Customer invokes search for hotel rooms option
  2. The system determines the customers location and brings up different hotels nearby.
  3. The system requests the user to enter number of people, the check in and out time and date.
  4. The system finds all different hotels based on requirements the customer has entered.
  5. Customer chooses hotel.
Alternate Scenario
  1. The system unable to determine customer location
  2. No available hotels nearby.
  3. The system unable to find hotels to match the customer requirements.
  4. Customer chooses not to book hotel room.
ConclusionsA customer is able to search for hotel rooms.
Post Conditions Customer can book hotel rooms.
Business Rules
  1. Customer must have an account.
  2. Customer must log on to make a purchase.
  3. In order to book hotel, customer must purchase a concert/performance ticket.
Implementation Constraints
