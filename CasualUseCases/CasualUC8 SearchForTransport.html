Casual Use Cases:

UC 8: Search for Transport

Use Case Number: 8
Use Case Name: Search for Transport
Description: A customer can choose:
  • Type of Transport
  • Date & time of departure
  • Date & time of Arrival
  • Location of venue
  • Location of hotel
Alternative Scenario:
  • No Transport for selected options chosen - shows up an error message
  • No transport options are available - shows up an error message
