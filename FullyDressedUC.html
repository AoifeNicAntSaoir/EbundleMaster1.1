Fully Dressed Use Case:

1. Account Sign up

Use Case Name:Account Sign Up
Use Case Id 1
Priority 1
Source
Primary Business Actory Customer
Other Participating Actors Database Administrator
Description A customer needs to create an account to in order to purchase tickets from the E-ticketing system.
Preconditions A customer must hold a valid email address
Trigger
Typical Scenario
  1. A customer requests a sign up for the EbundleSystem.
  2. The system prepares a form for sign up
  3. The customer inputs their email address, a username and a strong password (Mixed case, characters, letters and numbers)
  4. The system sends a confirmation email to the user's email address
  5. The system tells the customer to check their email address to verify their account with EbundleSystem
  6. The customer goes to their email, clicks on the link to validate their email
  7. The customer goes into the EbundleSystem website and requests to log in
Alternative Scenario
  1. Invalid password: customer is asked to re-enter their password.
  2. Invalid email: customer asked to enter a valid email address with username @ server . ext
  3. Existing account: if a customer's email address is already associated with an existing account, they are brought to the log-in page
  4. Validation Link Expires: need to try to log in again & go through forgot password process
ConclusionsA customer creates an account with EbundleSystem
Post ConditionsA customer can only create one account with one email address
Business Rules
  • A customer can only create one account with one email address
  • Validation link for validating new account only exists for 24 hours
Implementation Constraints




4.Search for concert/performance date

Use Case Name:Search for concert/performance date
Use Case Id 4
Priority 4
Source
Primary Business Actory Customer
Other Participating Actors
DescriptionCustomer search for dates of concerts/performances
Preconditions A customer must have an account and be logged in
Trigger
Typical Scenario
  1. A customer requests the search concert/performance date option
  2. The system determines the current date and select all the concerts /performances for up to 6 months in advance
  3. The customer can look at the events
  4. The customer can also specify a certain date, month of time to filter concerts
  5. The customer chooses to look at concerts
Alternative Scenario
  1. No concerts/performances available on date specified: The system returns with an error message & suggests other events
  2. Filtered dates do not return a search: The systems suggests other events
ConclusionsA customer is able to search for events on a specified date
Post ConditionsA customer can select a concert/perfomance to purchase tickets for etc.
Business Rules
  • The system will only bring up events 6 months in advance
  • The system cannot bring up bygone events
Implementation Constraints

7.Search for transport

Use Case Name:Search transport
Use Case Id 7
Priority 7
Source
Primary Business Actory Customer
Other Participating Actors
DescriptionCustomer searches for ltransport
Preconditions A customer must have an account and be logged in
Trigger
Typical Scenario
  1. A customer requests search for transport option
  2. The system determines the customers location & brings up different forms of transport nearby
  3. The system requests the user to enter the date and location of departure & arrival
  4. The system finds all different forms of transport with the corresponding costs, times on that date doing fitting as close as possible with the customer's requirements
  5. The customer chooses a form of transport
Alternative Scenario
  1. No transport available in location specified: The system returns with the nearest available transport to the specified location
  2. Filtered locations do not return a search: The systems suggests other events in nearby locations
  3. The system cannot determine the customers location
  4. The customer chooses not to purchase a transport option
ConclusionsA customer is able to search for events on a specified date
Post ConditionsA customer can book transport
Business Rules
  • The system will only bring up events 6 months in advance
  • The system cannot bring up bygone events
Implementation Constraints
