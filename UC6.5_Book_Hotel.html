Fully Dressed Use Case:

UC6.5: Book Hotel

Use Case Name:Book Hotel
Use Case Number 6.5
Priority 6
Source
Primary Business Actory Customer
Other Participating Actors System
DescriptionCustomer books the available hotel.
Preconditions Customer must log on first to book hotel.
Stakeholders & Interests
Customer:
Wants to be able to book hotel on the Ebundle System
Company:
Wants to make profit for the company
System:
Wants to be make sure that customer can successfully make a booking
Typical Scenario
  1. Customer log on to their account.
  2. Customer invokes search hotel
  3. Customer inputed required details to book hotel(ie location,check-in/out date,room type)
  4. Customer selects book now
  5. System adds the booking to cart.
Alternate Scenario
  1. Customer does not want to book hotel, in which case customer can exit the system.
ConclusionsBooking has been added to cart.
Post ConditionsCustomer confirm the booking by viewing the cart.
Business Rules
  1. Customer must have an account.
  2. Customer must log on to make a booking.
Implementation Constraints
