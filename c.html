Casual Use Cases:

Purchase concert/performance tickets

Use Case Number: 6
Use Case Name: Purchase concert/performance tickets
Success: When customer successfully logged in and added ticket to basket.
Alternative Scenario: When Tickets has been sold out, in which case customer will have to search for another concert/performance or exit system.
Cancelled concerts/performances, in which case customer will have to search for another concert/performance or exit system.


book Transport

Use Case Number: 8
Use Case Name: book Transport
Success: Customer successfully finds type of transport he/she wishes to book.
Alternative Scenario: The chosen type of transport is unavailable,in which case the customer must choose another type of transport or exit.
The customer does not want to book transport, in which the customer can exit the system.


Search Hotel Rooms

Use Case Number: 11
Use Case Name: Search Hotel Rooms
Success: When customer successfully logged in and added ticket to basket,there will be option search for hotel rooms within close proximity of the concert/performance venue.
Alternative Scenario:
When there is no available hotel rooms, the customer can search for other hotels.
The customer does not want to book for hotel rooms, in which case customer can directly go pay for the ticket or exit the system


Book Hotel

Use Case Number: 12
Use Case Name: Book Hotel
Success: Customer successfully searched for available hotel rooms, selects how many people and books the hotel room.
Alternative Scenario: Customer unable to search for available hotel rooms, in which case customer can search for another hotel or exit the system.
Customer does not want to book hotel rooms, in which case customer can exit the system.
